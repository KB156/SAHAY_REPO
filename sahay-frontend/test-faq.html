Account & Billing

To update your billing information, please go to the 'My Account' tab and select 'Payment Methods'.

You can reset your password by clicking the 'Forgot Password' link on the main login page.

General Questions

Our service is compatible with all modern web browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.

Downloads & Installation

You can find the download links for our desktop application in the 'Products' area of your dashboard.

The installer for Windows is named 'setup.exe' and the macOS version is 'Installer.dmg'.
